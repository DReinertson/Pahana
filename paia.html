Pa'ia

A small town located 25min away from our property. This small town has it all : Great shops for souveniers, some of our favorite restaurants, easy access beaches with pleanty of space and a grocery store so you can bring amazing treats back to the property to enjoy.

Ho'okipa Beach

Local beach, that's good for surfing and relaxing. Sea Turtles rest here at night. You can see as many as 30 turtles any given night. Section is barred off for them to come into and please respect the rules : Do not invade their space and do not touch the turtles. Trip Time : 20min from Pahana Farms
