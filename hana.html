The Road to Hana & Hana Town

Plan for an all day trip because once you're out in Hana, there is so much to do! From black / red sand beaches to 7-sacred pools, this small town will entertain for hours. Filled with watterfalls and views for days, even the drive itself is a site to see.

Activites

Red Sand Beach

Local beach, that's good for surfing and relaxing. Sea Turtles rest here at night. You can see as many as 30 turtles any given night. Section is barred off for them to come into and please respect the rules : Do not invade their space and do not touch the turtles. Trip Time : 20min from Pahana Farms
