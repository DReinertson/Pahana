The surrounding area

Pahana is located on the northeast shore of Maui. We recommend having a car to access all that Maui has to offer. Located on the road to hana, you're a short drive away from quaint little towns, amazing beaches and one of the most beautiful (and scary) drives you'll ever encounter.

Each town brings their own little spice to the adventure with their incredible food and drink. Ranging from crispy duck fajitas at Mambo's to upscale food with a view at Mama's Fish House, you'll be sure to leave with a full stomach.

Maui is also home to a few breweries and wineries. Kona brewing is set in a perfect space with plenty of room and games, equipped with food trucks coming in and out. Up country contains the wineries that will have you wanting more tastings and maybe even take a couple bottles home with you!
